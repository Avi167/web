Home Care: Your Role in the Outcome

No dental treatment achieves lasting results without active participation from the patient. The steps you take at home between appointments — brushing twice daily with fluoride toothpaste, flossing daily, maintaining a balanced diet, and staying hydrated — are as important as anything your dentist does in the chair. Our team provides personalized home care instructions at every appointment, tailored to your specific situation.

Equally important is attending regular maintenance appointments. Most patients benefit from a checkup and professional cleaning every six months. Those with specific risk factors related to dental implants may need more frequent visits to stay ahead of any developing issues.
