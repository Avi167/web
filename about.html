Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Care

Nims Dental Clinic was established with a single purpose: to make high-quality dental care accessible to every person in Pokhara, regardless of their background or budget. Located at Simalchour-8, our clinic has become a trusted healthcare destination for hundreds of families across Gandaki Pradesh.

From the very beginning, we have focused on combining modern dental technology with genuine compassion. Our patients are not just appointments on a schedule — they are members of our community, and every treatment decision is made with their long-term health and comfort in mind.

Today, Nims Dental Clinic proudly serves over 500 satisfied patients and continues to grow as Pokhara's most accessible and reliable dental clinic, open every day from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
